Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 29, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you use Resume Gen, we may collect:

  • Resume content you upload (PDF, DOCX, or TXT files)
  • Job posting text you provide
  • Generated resume and cover letter content

Automatically Collected Information

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and version
  • Usage patterns and preferences
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process your resume and job posting data to generate tailored resumes
  • Provide our AI-powered resume optimization service
  • Improve our service quality and user experience
  • Display relevant advertisements through our ad partners
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage and Processing

Resume Data: Your uploaded resume content is processed temporarily to generate the tailored resume. We do not permanently store your personal resume data on our servers.

AI Processing: Resume and job posting content is sent to Google's Gemini AI service for processing. Google's privacy policy applies to this data processing.

Local Processing: File parsing (PDF, DOCX) happens in your browser locally when possible.

4. Third-Party Services

AI Processing

We use Google's Gemini AI to process your resume and job posting data. Google's privacy policy governs this processing.

Advertising Partners

We work with advertising partners including:

  • Google AdSense: Displays targeted advertisements
  • Ezoic: Optimizes ad placements and manages ad serving

These partners may use cookies and similar technologies to collect information about your browsing behavior.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We and our partners use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze site usage and performance
  • Deliver personalized advertisements
  • Ensure privacy compliance (GDPR consent management)

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences or our consent management system.

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR and other privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your data (right to be forgotten)
  • Restrict processing of your data
  • Data portability
  • Object to processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure hosting infrastructure
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Access controls and authentication

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your residence, including the United States. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by posting the updated policy on our website.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Website: www.resume-gen.app

For privacy-related inquiries: Please use the contact information available on our website.
